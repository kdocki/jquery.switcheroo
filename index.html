You can use different toggles and combine them to make a powerful combination of display logic.

What is the order of execution though when you have multiple events?

The important thing to remember is the default event ordering is,

  1. toggle
  2. toggle-off
  3. toggle-on
Change eventOrdering for different event pattern

You can always change this in the defaults though by changing $.fn.switcheroo.defaults.eventOrdering
